A chronological look at every confirmed milestone for Avengers: Doomsday, from first announcement through to the December 2026 premiere.

Avengers: Doomsday officially releases in cinemas worldwide. The countdown above is ticking toward this date.

The first full-length theatrical trailer is expected ahead of the holiday release window, giving audiences a proper look at Robert Downey Jr. as Doctor Doom.
