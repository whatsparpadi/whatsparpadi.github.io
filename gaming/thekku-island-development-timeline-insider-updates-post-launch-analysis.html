Thekku Island: The Journey So Far

So, let's talk about Thekku Island, which is arriving on February 27, 2026.

Currently one of the most ambitious projects for all Malayali's (Kerala People), all of us feel a sense of pride and ownership about this game release.

Kerala's First 3D Horror Entry

This is indeed Kerala's first entry into the 3D Horror Theme as well as first ever entry on Steam library using the powerful Unreal Engine 5.

A Narrative Driven Experience

Thekku Island is a story-driven, first-person horror game where the protagonist, you, arrives on a remote island in search of your wife, only to discover something far worse. This narrative setup is beloved and follows the lineage of iconic survival horror games.

For example, in Resident Evil 7 Biohazard, the protagonist Ethan travels to a remote plantation after receiving a message from his missing wife, and you know the rest. Similarly, in Outlast 2, a journalist frantically searches for his missing pregnant wife in an isolated, fanatic village. Even in the psychological masterpiece Silent Hill 2, the protagonist receives a strange letter from his deceased wife, travels to an isolated misty town, and descends into darkness.

Assuming the game developers have played these games, they seem keen to present us a version rooted in our culture and our version of searching for a missing wife, to uncover the mystery that awaits us on the island.

The developers promise us that the game explores psychological horror, classic ghost encounters, and jumpscare tension, blending them into an atmospheric experience. Gameplay footage so far hints at solving puzzles or investigation of hidden clues rooted in Kerala’s rich folklore.

System Requirements & Optimization

To run the game, the minimum system requirements include Windows 10 64-bit, an Intel Core i5-6600K or AMD Ryzen 3 1200 CPU, 8GB RAM, an NVIDIA GTX 1650 or AMD RX 570 (4GB VRAM) GPU, and 8GB of storage.

Regarding performance, on February 21, 2026, within the official Discord's GENERAL-CHAT, two users—WPG Gaming and Sebastian Varkey—expressed concerns regarding the infamous optimization issues often associated with Unreal Engine 5 titles. The Game Director, Athul George, quickly reassured the community: the game is highly optimized to run smoothly on low-end PCs at 720p on low settings.

To put any lingering worries to rest, Athul George also noted that the development team themselves built the entire game using just a 4GB GPU!

Furthermore, the developers confirmed on Discord that the game will be priced under 500 Rupees ($6 in USD), making it accessible to a wider audience.

The Creative Minds Behind the Game

Another exciting aspect is the crew involved in the making of the game, featuring already popular talents. Aadhi Gopakumar, who was the primary vocalist for the hit track "Kathum Kaanal" in the movie Officer on Duty (2025) and handled Background Score & Programming for other movies including Thudarum (2025), works as the Music Director for this game. Next is Aswin Sunilkumar, who worked as a Lyrical Video Editor for Thudarum (2025), stepping in as the Creative Director for the game. Additionally, in the viral reveal trailer's background theme, the gripping violin hook is played by a friend, Anuroop Vijayan—a big shoutout to him!
